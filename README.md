## 📖 𝐓𝐡𝐞 𝐔𝐥𝐭𝐢𝐦𝐚𝐭𝐞 𝐎𝐓𝐀 & 𝐅𝐢𝐫𝐦𝐰𝐚𝐫𝐞 𝐆𝐮𝐢𝐝𝐞
*Are you tired of waiting for the staged rollout of the latest Nothing OS? Here is how you can use the files from Nothing Space to update your device manually.*

### Method 1: The Local Update Tool (Safest Method)
This method uses the hidden offline updater built into Nothing OS. It does not require a PC or unlocking the bootloader.
1. Download the correct **OTA Update ZIP** for your specific phone model from the [Nothing Space Firmware](https://nothingtools.github.io/).
2. Open your phone's File Manager and create a new folder named `ota` directly in the root of your internal storage.
3. Move the downloaded `.zip` file into this `ota` folder.
4. Open your phone's dialer app and type the following USSD code: `*#*#682#*#*`
5. The hidden **Offline Update Tool** will launch. It will automatically detect the ZIP file.
6. Tap on the file and select "Proceed". Do not restart your phone manually; it will reboot once the installation is complete.

### Method 2: ADB Sideloading (For Advanced Users)
Use this if your phone is stuck in a bootloop or you cannot access the OS.
1. Download the OTA ZIP to your PC from [Nothing Space](https://nothingtools.github.io/).
2. Install minimal ADB & Fastboot drivers on your PC.
3. Boot your Nothing Phone into **Recovery Mode** (Hold Volume Down + Power).
4. Select *“Apply update from ADB”*.
5. Connect your phone to the PC via the original Type-C cable.
6. Open Command Prompt on your PC and type: `adb sideload <filename>.zip`
7. Wait for the percentage to reach 100%, then select "Reboot system now".

## 🎨 𝐇𝐨𝐰 𝐓𝐨 𝐀𝐩𝐩𝐥𝐲 𝐃𝐞𝐩𝐭𝐡 𝐄𝐟𝐟𝐞𝐜𝐭 𝐊𝐋𝐖𝐏 𝐓𝐡𝐞𝐦𝐞𝐬
Give your Nothing Phone that premium iOS-style depth effect where the subject of the wallpaper overlaps the clock.
1. Install **KLWP Live Wallpaper Maker Pro** from the Google Play Store.
2. Download your favorite Depth Effect theme file (`.klwp`) from the [Nothing Space KLWP Themes](https://nothingtools.github.io/).
3. Open the KLWP app, grant necessary storage and location permissions.
4. Tap the menu icon (top left) -> **Load Preset**.
5. Tap the folder icon at the top and locate the downloaded `.klwp` file from Nothing Space.
6. Once loaded, tap the "Save" icon (floppy disk) at the top right.
7. Set KLWP as your default Home and Lock Screen wallpaper. Enjoy the magic!

## ❓ 𝐅𝐫𝐞𝐪𝐮𝐞𝐧𝐭𝐥𝐲 𝐀𝐬𝐤𝐞𝐝 𝐐𝐮𝐞𝐬𝐭𝐢𝐨𝐧𝐬 (𝐌𝐞𝐠𝐚 𝐅𝐀𝐐)

**Q1: What exactly is Nothing Space?** **A:** Nothing Space (`nothingtools.github.io`) is a community-driven, all-in-one web portal. We provide everything a Nothing or CMF device owner needs: OTA updates, firmware files, KLWP themes, GCam ports, service center locators, and P2P file-sharing tools.

**Q2: Are the OTA updates and firmware files safe?** **A:** Yes! All OTA ZIP files linked on Nothing Space are sourced directly from Nothing Technology Limited's official servers or trusted community dumps. We provide MD5 checksums so you can verify the integrity of the file before flashing.

**Q3: Does updating manually via Nothing Space void my warranty?** **A:** No. Sideloading official Nothing OS OTA files using the built-in offline updater (`*#*#682#*#*`) is an official method and does not void your warranty or wipe your data. However, unlocking your bootloader will.

**Q4: Why am I getting an "Update Installation Failed" error?** **A:** This usually happens if you try to flash a downgrade package over a newer OS without wiping data, or if you downloaded the wrong region's firmware (e.g., flashing Global firmware on an EEA device). Always double-check your model number on Nothing Space.

**Q5: How does the File Drop (P2P) feature work?** **A:** It uses WebRTC. When you share a file, it creates a direct, encrypted tunnel between your device and the receiver's device. The file is never uploaded to Nothing Space servers, ensuring 100% privacy and infinite file size limits.

**Q6: Can I submit my own custom wallpaper or KLWP setup?** **A:** Absolutely. We are "Built Together". You can submit your setups, widgets, or wallpapers through our GitHub repository by opening a Pull Request, or via the submission form on the site.

**Q7: Do you have tools for the new CMF Phone 1?** **A:** Yes! Nothing Space fully supports the CMF Phone 1. We have dedicated sections for CMF wallpapers, firmware, and guides on how to 3D print custom backplates.

**Q8: Are the Camera Presets better than the stock Nothing Camera?** **A:** Stock camera is great, but our GCam (Google Camera) XML presets are tweaked specifically for Nothing’s IMX sensors to provide better HDR+, natural skin tones, and superior low-light astrophotography.

**Q9: How do I find the nearest Nothing Service Center?** **A:** Simply navigate to the Locator tool on [Nothing Space](https://nothingtools.github.io/). Select your state and city, and it will instantly provide the address, phone number, and Google Maps directions to the nearest authorized service center in India.

**Q10: Is this website officially affiliated with Nothing Tech?** **A:** No. Nothing Space is an independent, open-source, community-led project created by enthusiasts (shoutout to Rohit). We are not officially affiliated with Carl Pei or Nothing Technology Limited. All copyrights belong to their respective owners.

Having issues? Check our quick troubleshooting guide before heading to the service center.

* **Bootloop after OTA Update:** If your phone is stuck on the Nothing logo, boot into Recovery Mode (Volume Down + Power) and select "Wipe Data / Factory Reset". *Note: This will erase local data.*
* **Offline Updater not showing ZIP:** Ensure the file is named ending in `.zip` and is placed exactly inside a folder named `ota` in the root directory. Ensure the file isn't corrupted by checking the file size.
* **KLWP touch actions not working:** Make sure you have removed the stock app icons from your home screen. KLWP elements sit behind standard Android icons.
